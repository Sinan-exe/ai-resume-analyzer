# 🚀 AI Resume Analyzer

A modern AI-powered web application that helps job seekers analyze their resumes and improve their chances of landing interviews.

🔗 **Live Demo:** https://ai-resume-analyzer-ae-mu.vercel.app/

---

## ✨ Features

- 📄 **Resume Upload**
  - Upload your resume in PDF format for analysis

- 🤖 **AI-Powered Analysis**
  - Get intelligent feedback on your resume structure and content

- 📊 **ATS Score & Optimization**
  - Understand how well your resume performs with Applicant Tracking Systems

- 🎯 **Job Description Matching**
  - Compare your resume against job descriptions for better alignment

- 🛠️ **Actionable Suggestions**
  - Receive clear improvements to enhance your resume quality

- ⚡ **Fast Processing**
  - Instant feedback with a smooth user experience

---

## 🧠 How It Works

1. Enter job details (company, role, description)
2. Upload your resume
3. The system:
   - Converts your resume into an image
   - Uploads and processes it
   - Uses AI to analyze and generate feedback

4. View detailed insights and suggestions
